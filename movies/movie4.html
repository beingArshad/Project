Aquaman

Director: James Wan
IMDb Rating: 7.6/10⭐

Aquaman is a 2018 American superhero film based on the DC Comics character of the same name. It is the second installment in the DC Extended Universe (DCEU) and was directed by James Wan. The film stars Jason Momoa as Arthur Curry / Aquaman, with Amber Heard, Patrick Wilson, Michael Caine, and Temuera Morrison in supporting roles. The story follows Aquaman as he must unite the underwater kingdoms to defend against an ancient threat. The film received generally positive reviews and was a commercial success, grossing over $1.1 billion worldwide.
