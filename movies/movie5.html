500 Days of Summer

Director: Marc Webb
IMDb Rating: 7.6/10⭐

500 Days of Summer is a 2009 American romantic comedy-drama film written and directed by Marc Webb. The film stars Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Tom Hansen and Zooey Deschanel as Summer Wheatley. The story follows the relationship between Tom and Summer over the course of 500 days, exploring themes of love, loss, and self-discovery. The film received positive reviews for its authentic portrayal of young love and the chemistry between the lead actors. It has since gained a cult following for its unique narrative structure and emotional depth.
